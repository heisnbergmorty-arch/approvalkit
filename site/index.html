FAQ

Who is this for?

Small design agencies and freelance designers (1–20 people) doing branding, web, or product design where every project has 2–5 approval cycles with the client.

How is this different from Figma comments or Loom?

Figma is for designers, not for clients. Most clients won't open Figma, won't comment in it, and won't approve there. ApprovalKit is built for the client side: a branded link they can open from their phone, see the work, leave one comment, and approve. No Figma account needed.

How is this different from SuperOkay or ManyRequests?

Those are full agency-management platforms (requests, billing, CRM, time tracking). They include some approval features as one of many. ApprovalKit does ONE thing — design approval cycles — and does it better for design agencies specifically.

Do I need to be a developer to set this up?

You need to be comfortable with the command line for ~30 minutes. The docs walk you through every step. If you've ever deployed a Next.js app, you'll be fine. If not, the Pro plan includes setup support.

What if I don't like it?

14-day no-questions refund. Email us within 14 days, get your money back.

Will I get updates?

Yes — lifetime free updates included in both plans.
